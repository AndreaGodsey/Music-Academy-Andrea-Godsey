Leopold Corvette-Guitar Instructor

Teaching Philosophy: Students become masters through passion and practice!

Musical Proficiency: Guitar, Bass

Leopold Corvette may be our oldest instructor, but his wisdom has inspired several musicians of our time! Back in the 70's, Leopold found his personality through guitar. After dropping out of college to begin his solo career, Leopold became famous for countless songs about love and loss. His songs have always been described as nostalgic and passionate, moving many of his audiences to tears even to this day. Now at the age of 65, Leopold has entered retirement, becoming one of our most popular instructors at the academy! When he isn't playing guitar, he is currently practicing the bass, and is always willing to give advice on how to play the instrument. He is always at the library, reading a variety of books and attends many book clubs to discuss his interests. He always welcomes students to attend with him.
