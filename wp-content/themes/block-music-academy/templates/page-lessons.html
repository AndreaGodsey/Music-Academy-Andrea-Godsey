Music Lessons/Trial Courses

Instruments Currently Offered

Note: We only offer a maximum of two lessons per week. Each lesson can only be a maximum two hours per day, to give other students time to learn from their instructors.

  • Piano
  • Violin
  • Drums
  • Guitar

Piano

Skill Level: Intermediate

Price(Per Hour): $55.00

Ever hear the soft notes of the piano and often wonder if you could play something similar? Already know the basics of the instrument and want to jump into playing actual songs? Meet with our favorite piano instructor Collete Heavensby! During her lessons, you will be taught to play short songs, skipping over the basics you have already come to know! After Collete's lessons, you'll be able to play the more advanced songs you have always dreamed of learning! Due to the skill level of the course, Collete is not currently offering trial courses. Note: You can only have two lessons per week, and each lesson can only be a maximum of two hours long.

Violin

Skill Level: Advanced

Price(Per Hour): $60.00

Do you enjoy listening to Beethoven's musical numbers in your spare time? Ever wondered what it would be like to play those same famous songs yourself? Have already an understanding of the instrument and sheet music, and are just looking for some pointers from an advanced instructor? Meet with Lucy Hollander, our famous violin instructor! Lucy is known for her stricter techniques, but there has never been an unsatisfied student in the past! In Lucy's lessons, you learn more advanced techniques with the violin, as well as more difficult songs to practice to your hearts content! Due to the skill level of this course, there is currently not a trial course offered with Lucy at this time. Note: You can only have two lessons per week, and each lesson can only be a maximum of two hours long.

Drums

Skill Level: Beginner

Price(Per Hour): $50.00

Ever heard the drums and wondered if you could ever play the instrument? Meet with our newest instructor, Paul Henderson! Paul is currently accepting students of all varying skill levels in music. During his lessons, you can learn how to read the sheet music, and how to keep time with the rhythm of your practice songs. In no time, you will go from playing basic notes, to playing long-winded drum solos! Paul Henderson is also offering trial courses, so you can freely play the drums to see if the fun instrument is right for you! Note: You can only have two lessons per week, and each lesson can only be a maximum of two hours long.

Guitar

Skill Level: Beginner

Price(Per Hour): $50.00

Have you ever felt a special connection to the guitar? Ever listen to acoustic versions of your favorite songs, and wonder if you can play the notes? Learn to play guitar from our very own Leopold Corvette! Leopold offers lessons for students that are new to the musical world. He discusses the basics of sheet music, while teaching his students the importance of rhythm. You will be taught how to play basic notes in the beginning to eventually playing short songs. Leopold is also accepting trial courses, so you can freely test out the guitar to see if it suits your musical passions. Note: You can only have two lessons per week, and each lesson can only be a maximum of two hours long.
